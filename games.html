Game Design

The following is my first foray into game design. It was adapted from a tutorial on Codeacademy.com. It is dedicated to my co-worker and brother-from-another-mother Sean.

The game is a text-based, prompt-driven adventure. The main character is Sean, a retail worker, who is just trying to finish his job without any interference from customers. Although it is short, you have several options of how to navigate your way through the customer interaction.

Please enjoy, and just refresh the page to try again and test you willpower against the mighty and terrifying "customer."
