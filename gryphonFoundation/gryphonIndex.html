Gryphon: A 1970's Musical Gem

The Renaissance Rock Band

The 1970's birthed some of the most intricate and exploratory rock music ever created. Progressive rock's influences were plentiful, although Classical art music is the most researched.

What set Gryphon apart was their firm grounding in the styles of Renaissance and folk music. Seriously, who else is going to write a rock song with krummhorns and recorders? No one!

You'll Wish You'd Known Them Sooner

If you enjoy folk music, rock music, classical music, Renaissance music, or just music in general, you MUST check out this band.
